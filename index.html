About me:


Since 2015, I am Research Assistant at the Artificial Intelligence and Computer Science Research Lab (LIACC) at Faculty of Engineering of the University of Porto (FEUP).


In July 2016, I received my M.Sc. in Informatics and Computing Engineering (MIEIC) from the Faculty of Engineering of the University of Porto (FEUP), with a thesis on mining argumentative content from textual resources.


Research interests: Machine Learning, Natural Language Processing, Argumentation Mining, Computational Semantics, Transfer Learning, Cross-Language Learning, Knowledge Representation and Reasoning, and Multi-Agent Systems.
